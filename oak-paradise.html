How We Improved Our Engagement Twitter/Discord?

Began with a Twitter Space AMA for the community, co-hosted by CryptoApe, where the founder was able to share his story and vision for Oak

Leveraged Oak’s talented art studio for many illustrations throughout our engagement for Twitter and created many 1/1 NFTs for influencers and project founders

MultiChain also helped run their social media accounts.

Twitter Growth: 100k -> 150k (peak hype pre-mint)

Consistent transparency on Discord coming directly from the Founder with various announcements and milestones

Discord Growth: 0 -> 30k (peak hype pre-mint)

Held Discord AMAs leading up to mint, so the community can ask questions in a more private environment

How We Addressed FUD?

- Our team of 8+ moderators working around the clock and answering all questions from the community

New, Unique, Revenue
Share System

To be eligible for revenue share from the sportsbook, casino, and poker platform, you must:
- Own 1 legendary Oak Paradise NFT
- Own 3 Oak Paradise NFTs
- Own an NFT from the selected trait for the week

This drove people to want to own more than one NFT and created a large demand for legendaries

It even allowed people to take a risk and only own one or two for a chance to be part of the selected trait of the week
