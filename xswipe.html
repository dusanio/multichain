All You Need To Know About Xswipe

Issues with Magic Eden

Xswipe was originally going to mint on August 18th, 2022 on Magic Eden, but three days prior to their mint they were dropped from their launchpad after sending bank documents.

Xswipe never violated any terms set in their contract and provided all necessary documents asked by Magic Eden.

After being contacted by Xswipe, Multichain conducted thorough due diligence on their banking relationships and documents and began our month- long engagement

Problem 1

Being dropped as a client from the leading launchpad in the ecosystem drastically harmed their reputation.

Problem 2

With Solana being such a fast paced environment, Solswipe needed to get organized and stop the FUD revolving around them.

How did Multichain Help?

Led rebranding and marketing strategy.

Formulated the mint strategy, including
the presale idea and executed on it.

Handled entire whitelist collaboration
strategy with 250+ DAOs and NFT
projects on Solana and Ethereum.

Marketing & Rebranding

After Multichain conducted our due diligence on Xswipe's banking documents and their doxxing, Multichain publicly vouched for Xswipe's product, team, and vision.

Scheduled many Twitter spaces and Discord AMAs throughout our engagement with top communities in the ecosystem.

Cryptoape, Multichain's CEO & Founder, served as a beta tester of Xswipe's physical debit card and connected Xswipe with various other beta testers.

Whitelist Collaborations

Multichain handled the whitelist collaboration process for Xswipe with over 100+ collabs with top DAOs across Solana and Ethereum.

Mint Strategy (Presale)

Presales are now a popular strategy due to the additional FOMO it creates caused by the lower supply on mint date and typically attracting high quality investors.

Multichain proposed and led the presale stage of Xswipe's mint, including vetting and contacting all presale buyers.
