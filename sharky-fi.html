What is Sharky?

Sharky is a platform where anyone can make loan offers for NFTs. Holders can choose the NFT and instantly borrow $SOL. You make money as a lender and can get cash-on-demand as a borrower. Sharky strives to bridge DeFi with NFTs while making it simple and fun to borrow and lend on Solana.

How Do Lenders Benefit
From Using Sharky?

When lenders offer SOL, they will get high interest rates with high APY when they lend or if the borrower fails to repay the loan, the lender will become the owner of the NFT.

Once the offer is accepted, the borrower receives the lender's $SOL and their NFT is locked until they repay the loan or it defaults and is automatically sent to the lender.
